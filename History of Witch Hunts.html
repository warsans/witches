Witch Persecution

      Firstly, witches were accused of working with the devil which specifically goes against their puritan values. The hunt began after a series of suspicions and rumors. Witch Hunts were seen across Europe in countries like Germany, Italy, France and Switzerland. In the European witch hunts it is estimate dthat over 100,000 people were accused of witchcraft and nearly half of that had been executed.

With no separation between church and state, members of authority used their puritan values to prosecute the accused.
