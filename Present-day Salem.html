Salem, Massachusetts
      In October, Salem, Massachusetts receives over a million tourists for Haunted Happenings. According to TripsToDiscover.com, Salem was considered the beest Halloween event in the U.S. and one of the best places to travel worldwide in October. The city announces the road closures and detour prior to celebrations with knowledge that they will receive a large influx of visitors.
